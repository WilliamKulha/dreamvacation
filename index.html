Kyoto

You can't see Japan until you've visited it's cultural heart.

Kyoto 京都

Kyoto, located in the Kansai region, is one of Japan's oldest cities. It was the Imperial capital for over 1,000 years and has the heritage architecture to prove it.

Places to visit:

  • The Temple of the Golden Pavilion
  • Fushimi Inari Shrine
  • Kiyomizu Temple
  • Gion
  • The Temple of the Silver Pavilion

What to Eat if you like Japanese food

  • Sou: For Wagyu Grade A5 beef
  • Chihana: For kaiseki, or Japanese Haute cuisine
  • Hafuu: For surf and turf, Washoku style
  • Nishiki Warai: For okonomiyaki
  • Ippudo Nishikikouji: For ramen
